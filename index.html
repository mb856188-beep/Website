About Leeza

Leeza Cook is a professionally trained violinist who has been playing since the age of 10, tutored at Burnt Mill Music School and performing with ensembles from an early age, joined Harlow Youth Orchestra at the age of 12 where the orchestra was conducted by a member of the renowned Alberni String Quartet.

With years of experience across weddings, concerts, and private events, Leeza blends strong technical grounding with a warm, expressive tone to create an atmosphere that is elegant, heartfelt, and deeply personal.

From an intimate ceremony to a relaxed garden reception, the focus is always on music that supports the mood, feels natural within your setting, and reflects your event.

Duo option

Whistler’s Mother

For larger receptions or events, Leeza also performs as part of Whistler's Mother, a modern violin + accompanist duo offering an expanded, richer sound and specialising in blues/rock folk fusion + Irish folk.

Ideal for wedding breakfasts, and bespoke events where you would like a fuller musical backdrop while still keeping things refined and conversational.
