About Leeza Cook

Leeza Cook is a professionally trained violinist who has been playing since the age of 10 - tutored at Burnt Mill Music School and performing with ensembles from an early age. She joined Harlow Youth Orchestra at the age of 12 where the orchestra was conducted by a member of the renowned Alberni String Quartet. With years of experience across private events, Leeza blends strong technical grounding with a warm, expressive tone to create an atmosphere that is elegant, heartfelt, and deeply personal.


From an intimate ceremony to a relaxed garden reception, the focus is always on music that supports the mood, feels natural within your setting, and reflects your event.
